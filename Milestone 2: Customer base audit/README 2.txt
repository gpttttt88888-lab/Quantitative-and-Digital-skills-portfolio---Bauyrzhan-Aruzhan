# Quantitative and Digital Skills Portfolio – Bauyrzhan Aruzhan


## Project Overview
This repository demonstrates my quantitative and digital skills through customer transaction data analysis. The analyses include deciles, cohorts, comparative cohort analysis, and yearly summaries to derive business insights from revenue, profit, and customer trends.


## Datasets
- *Deciles*: Customer segmentation based on revenue and profit.
- *Cohort*: Quarterly analysis of customer activity and transactions.
- *3 Cohorts*: Comparative analysis of three cohorts.
- *Year summaries*: Aggregated revenue, profit, and transactions per year and cohort.


## Analyses and Visualizations
1. *Deciles Analysis*
   - Bar charts: Revenue and profit by decile
   - Pie charts: Contribution of each decile to total profit


2. *Cohort Analysis*
   - Line charts: Customer activity, revenue, and profit over time


3. *3 Cohorts*
   - Comparative charts showing revenue and profit trends


4. *Yearly Summaries*
   - Bar and line charts: Yearly revenue, profit, customers, and transactions


## Key Insights
- Top deciles contribute the majority of revenue and profit.
- Customer and transaction trends vary quarterly and yearly.
- Cohort comparisons reveal differences in revenue and profit contribution.
- Long-term trends highlight business growth and opportunities.


## Tools Used
- Google Sheets for data analysis and visualization
- Pivot tables and charts
